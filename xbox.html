Xbox Pros and Cons

The Pros

  • Xbox Game Pass: In a nutshell this is the main reason to own an xbox. The pass allows you to play a bunch of games, gives you a tons of discounts and perks. Too many to name even. It's also a great deal because it costs only $10.
  • Great resolution and refresh rate: Supports 8k resolution and 120 hz refresh rate.
  • Backwards compatible: Most of the greatest games from the old generation are still available on the Xbox Series X/S

The Cons

  • Lack of Exclusives: The Xbox only games are very few since most of the exclusive series already wrapped up their stories.
  • Lacks extras: No motion in controls, or speakers and no VR capabilities. The Xbox does what it does and nothing more.
  • UI clutter: The menu to get around the xbox is a little messy and confusing to navigate at times.
