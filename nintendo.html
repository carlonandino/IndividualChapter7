Nintendo Switch Pros and Cons

The Pros

  • Price: Literally the cheapest of the big three consoles at $300.
  • The Games: Exclusive games and is the most group oriented of all the consoles. Fun for friends and family.
  • Features: Motion controls, tons of control add-ons and fun accessories and the console is also a HANDHELD!

The Cons

  • Controls: The controllers motion stick stops working pretty often and isnt that easy to fix. Will always be buying new controls.
  • No Chat: The consoles doesn't have a built in way to talk with friends so communication is usually text or preselected phrases.
  • Battery life: The console dies the batteries die, everything is always dead unless you have it forever plugged in.
