Playstation Pros and Cons

The Pros

  • Exclusive Games: The playstation right now has some of the best exclusive games. Great games like Spiderman, God of War, and The Last of Us.
  • Great resolution and refresh rate: Supports 8k resolution and 120 hz refresh rate.
  • Backwards compatible: Most of the greatest games from the PS4 generation are still available on the Playstation 5.

The Cons

  • Most expensive: The most expensive of the big three consoles at $500.
  • Design: The weird shape of the console makes it look good but makes it hard to put in lots of areas.
  • Controllers: Again a good but bad thing. Interesting touch pad, motion, and speaker system but then there is the triggers which are so sensitive that putting the control down anywhere activates them.
